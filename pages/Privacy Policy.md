<h4><strong>6. Use of Cookies, Web Beacons, and Similar Technologies on the Website</strong></h4>
<p>6.1 When you visit, access or interact with the SGGC website, we or our authorised service providers may use cookies, web beacons, and other similar technologies for collecting and storing information to help provide you with a better, faster, and safer web experience.</p>
<p>6.2 The information collected by us or our authorised service providers may recognise a visitor as a unique user and may collect information such as how a visitor arrives at our websites, what kind of browser a visitor is on, what operating system a visitor is using, a visitor’s IP address and a visitor’s click stream information and time stamp (for example, which pages they have viewed, the time the pages were accessed and the time spent per web page).</p>
<p>6.3 The use of cookies, web beacons and similar technologies by us on our website perform different functions. They are either necessary for the functioning of our services, help us improve our performance, or serve to provide you with extra functionalities.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 20px;">6.3.1 <strong>Cookies</strong> - Small text files (typically made up of letters and numbers) placed in the memory of your browser or device when you visit a website or view a message. Cookies allow a website to recognise a particular device or browser. There are several types of cookies:</p>
<p style="margin-left: 40px;">(a) Session cookies expire at the end of your browser session and allow us to link your actions during that particular browser session.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 40px;">(b) Persistent cookies are stored on your device in between browser sessions, allowing us to remember your preferences or actions across multiple sites.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 40px;">(c) First-party cookies are set by the site you are visiting.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 40px;">(d) Third-party cookies are set by a third party site separate from the site you are visiting.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 40px;">Cookies can be disabled or removed by tools that are available in most commercial browsers. The preferences for each browser you use will need to be selected separately and different browsers offer different functionalities and options. You can find out more about cookies at www.allaboutcookies.org.</p>

<p style="margin-left: 20px;">6.3.2 <strong>Web Beacons</strong> - Small graphic images (also known as “pixel tags” or “clear GIFs”) may be included on our websites and services. Web beacons typically work in conjunction with cookies to profile each unique user and user behaviour.</p>

<p style="margin-left: 20px;">6.3.3 <strong>Similar Technologies</strong> - Technologies that store information in your browser or device utilising local shared objects or local storage, such as flash cookies, HTML 5 cookies, and other web application software methods. These technologies can operate across all of your browsers.</p>
<p>6.4 We offer certain website features and services that are available only through the use of these technologies. You are always free to block, delete, or disable these technologies if your browser so permits.</p>
<p>6.5 However, if you decline cookies or other similar technologies, you may not be able to take advantage of certain website features or services tools. For more information on how you can block, delete, or disable these technologies, please review your browser settings.</p>
<p>6.6 The SGGC website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of our website by storing, generally non-personally identifiable, information (e.g. session information such as the number of times you visit our website and the location from which you are accessing the website). Note that some limited information is personally identifiable for the purposes of:</p>
<p style="margin-left: 20px;">6.6.1 managing and customising your experience on the website;</p>
<p style="margin-left: 20px;">6.6.2 analysing trends and improving our site for our users; and</p>
<p style="margin-left: 20px;">6.6.3 delivering content tailored to your interests and the manner in which you browse our website and platforms,</p>
<p>and will be treated accordingly as Personal Data by SGGC acting in compliance with this Privacy Policy and applicable law.</p>
